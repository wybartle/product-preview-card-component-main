Aia kekahi mau kānaka o Hawaiʻi nei ma ka Hoʻokūkū Hīmeni, ʻo Ka Leo

Ma ka puni mua o ka Hoʻokūkū Hīmeni ʻo Ka Leo, lanakila aku nei nā puʻukani ʻelua o Hawaiʻi. ʻO Kaylee Shimizu ka mua. No ʻEwa mai ʻo ia a he ʻumi kumamāhiku ona makahiki. Aia ʻo ia ma ka hui a John Legend. A ʻo ka lua, ʻo Jason Arcilla. No Pukalani, Maui mai ʻo ia. A he kanakolu kumamāhā ona makahiki. Aia ʻo Arcilla ma ka hui a Gwen Stefani. E hoʻomaka ana ka puni ʻelua i ka Pōʻakahi.

ʻO nā mea ʻekolu i koe e kau ana i ka hano hāweo ma muli o kā lākou hana ma ka ʻoihana ʻoki leo, ʻo ia ʻo Karen Keawehawaiʻi, ʻo Audy Kimura, a ʻo Cynthia “Cindy” Ann Lance. ʻO Keawehawaiʻi, he lālā ʻo ia ma ka pāna nā Kāhuna ʻAkaʻaka i nā makahiki 1970. ʻO ia ka wā i hoʻomaka ai kona hīmeni ʻana i mua o ka lehulehu.
